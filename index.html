Undergraduate Thesis

Title: Strategic approach for extraction and purification of peroxidase enzyme from horseradish roots, structural and functional characterization, with application in enzymatic phenol degradation and antimicrobial activity

Supervised by: Dr. Mahbuba Jannat | Partner: Swarnila Chakma | Mentor: Pritam Hasan

Timeline: Jan 2024 – Mar 2025

Details: Extraction and purification of horseradish peroxidase (HRP). FTIR and SEM analyses confirmed structure. UV-Vis assays: optimal at pH 7.8, 37°C. Phenol degradation improved 5.6% → 20.1%. Antimicrobial activity against E. coli (6.5–10 mm inhibition).
